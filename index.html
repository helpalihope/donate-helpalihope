The Disaster: Treasure NFT Fraud

In 2024, Treasure NFT emerged as a "revolutionary" project, promising huge profits and luring thousands with fake success stories.

What Happened:

  • Marketed as Pakistan's first major NFT platform
  • Promised 300–500% returns in months
  • Convinced young investors with fake testimonials
  • In March 2025, the founders vanished with investors' money
  • Pakistani media called it the country's biggest crypto scam
  • Thousands lost their life savings

My Involvement:
